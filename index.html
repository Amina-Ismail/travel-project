Why South Africa?

South Africa, The Rainbow Nation

South Africa is known for it's diverse culture, natural beauty and it;'s varied topography. South Africa is the only country in the world known to have three capital cities.When visiting South Africa, expect stunning coastlines, dramatic mountainscapes, huge areas of untamed bushveld, starkly beautiful semi-deserts, lakes, waterfalls, spectacular canyons, forests, wide plains and the list goes on.

South Africa is a beautiful country with a rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes. The colorful mix of cultures give South Africa it's nickname "rainbow nation". South African's are passionate about music, and use songs and dance to express their social and political ideas. South Africa offers a huge variety of breathtaking holidays and experiences throughout the year.

Cities

The three largest cities

Durban

Durban is South Africa's third-largest City and one of the fastest-growing urban Areas in the World. Durban has some of the best beaches in South Africa. Durban is blessed with beautiful people, humid weather and a warm current in the Ocean, making it the 'perfect holiday paradise!'

Learn more

Johannesburg

Johannesburg is a mulitcultural city and is ranked top in Africa. The city has fantastic weather, a variety of activities and not forgetting affordable accommodation. It has a unique geography that combines beauty and mineral wealth, attracting people from near and far. Pay a visit.

Learn more

Cape Town

The country's oldest city.Centred around Table Mountain, the Mother City is known for its beautiful beaches, stunning biodiversity, award-winning food and wine, and A sheer variety of experiences. This coastal gem has been voted the best city in the world seven-years-running!
